Why dogs are better than cats

Introduction

This page aims to provide clear arguments in support of the above statement.

The better pet

Human beings choose to have pets for many reasons including:
  • companionship
  • excuses to exercise
  • having something to care for that isn't another human being or a plant

Dog vs. cat

Dogs are therefore better because...
  1. they are needy enough to provide proper companionship
  2. you have to take them for walks
  3. they require a lot of looking after and are not self-sufficient, thus fulfilling the desire to care for something
